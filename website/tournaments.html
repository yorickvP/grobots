Grobots - Tournaments

We hold tournaments from time to time. The results, except for the formalized Top Nine tournament, are reported here. If you want your sides included, send them to us.

The analysis page may be useful for understanding the results.

There are separate Newbie Tournaments, for people who have never had a side in the top ten or so.

Grudge's early score is better than Gunner's. Significantly so. This means provoking enemies is now a bigger problem than ignoring them, at least in the beginning. It doesn't do so well later.

All the gatherers, except Segregated Eaters, have a late death rate more than three times their early death rate. This is partly because their early death rates overstate their early performance, and partly because they're unable to concentrate enough force to win battles in the late game. A wandering gatherer might be improved by deliberately keeping its army in a group.

Error

(20 Nov 2005) This was a bit bigger than usual - 28 sides for 600 rounds (10626346 frames in 41296 s = 257 fps). Error is still pretty big. This table is modified from the output of headless mode. The survival score includes the sterile case, so Not Quite Harmless scores very high.

  • Business Cycle keeps getting better.
  • Wise Men's Jose seems to have been a waste of energy. Making more fighters instead greatly improves it.
  • Segregated Eaters avoid competing for food by having each gatherer use a different 10x10 tile.
  • Shepherds is unarmed, but its forcefields and running away protect it from most enemies (except Business Cycle).
  • Weird Men uses enemy-syphons to weaken its enemies.
  • Noffee 4 has bigger Escherichias so they can move while pregnant with a heavy NoFF.
  • Grudge 4 fixes bugs but is still worse than Gunner, especially early. A similar experiment with Eventually gave the same result.
